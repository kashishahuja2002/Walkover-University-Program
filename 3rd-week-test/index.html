Mixins

Mixins are a great feature of SCSS. Mixins are just like functions in any programming language. Mixins allow you to define styles that can be re-used throughout your stylesheet. They make it easy to avoid using non-semantic classes like .float-left, and to distribute collections of styles in libraries.

Syntax:- @mixin name(arguments...) {....}

To use it after declaration,
Syntax:- @include name(arguments...)
